Connection Matters

92% of elders feel happier after spending time with younger generations.

Activities to Do Together

Simple ways to bridge the generational gap and create lasting memories.

Wellness Walks

Take a gentle stroll in a local park. It's great for physical health and provides a perfect setting for conversation.

Heritage Cooking

Learn a family recipe. It preserves culture and ensures your loved one is eating nutritious meals.

Tech Support

Help them navigate video calls or health apps. It reduces isolation and keeps them connected.

Practical Tips for Caregivers

1

Empathy-Based Communication

Listen more than you speak. Use a calm, respectful tone and maintain eye level when talking.

2

Monitor Physical Safety

Check for trip hazards at home. Ensure they have comfortable, non-slip footwear and adequate lighting.

3

Social Engagement

Encourage participation in community centers. Social health is just as important as physical health.
