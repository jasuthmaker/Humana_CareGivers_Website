Physical Wellness for Older Adults

Small daily habits can protect strength, balance, and independence.

Caregivers can support healthy movement, better nutrition, medication routines, and safer homes—without making elders feel controlled or rushed.

Try this week’s action

Move Together

Plan 20–30 minute low-impact activities like neighborhood walks, chair stretches, or gentle dance. Consistency matters more than intensity.

Safer Home Setup

Reduce fall risks: clear walking paths, add night lights, keep commonly used items within reach, and use non-slip mats in bathrooms.

Nutrition Support

Build simple meal plans with protein, colorful vegetables, hydration reminders, and easy-prep snacks to avoid skipped meals.

Care Routine

Use a visible checklist for medications, checkups, and sleep routines. Invite elders to co-create the schedule for shared control.

Weekly Family Challenge

Choose one day this week to do a “wellness hour” together: a short walk, healthy meal prep, and a quick home safety check.
